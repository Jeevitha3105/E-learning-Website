FAQ

Can I access course materials offline?

Currently, our platform requires an internet connection to access course materials. However, you can save course materials for offline viewing by downloading them through our mobile app, available on iOS and Android devices.

What types of payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. Additionally, we support payments through PayPal for added convenience.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in courses?

Prerequisites vary depending on the course. Each course description will specify any recommended or required prerequisites. If you have questions about whether you meet the prerequisites for a course, please contact our support team for assistance.

How long do I have access to course materials after enrolling?

Once enrolled in a course, you have unlimited access to the course materials for as long as the course remains available on our platform. This includes any updates or additions made to the course content over time.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with a course, you may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact our support team with your request, and we'll process it promptly.

Do you offer certificates upon course completion?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for many of our courses. Once you finish a course, you can download and print your certificate directly from your account dashboard. Certificates typically include the course name, completion date, and instructor's name.
